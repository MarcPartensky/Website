About Me

Learning everyday because life is too short to waste any.

Royan, France - August 2020

My name is Marc Partensky.

I am 20 years old and I study at ISEP.

  • Actively looking for a job.
  • Hyperactive on Github.
  • 5 certificates in Deep learning
  • Fluent in English

I am used to spending most of my time coding on my multiple projects. I started coding at the age of 16 on my calculator. Then at the age of 18 I started coding with Python which quickly became my favorite programming language. I began by playing with pygame and even later made my own library on top of it named pygame-geometry which was used for most of my pygame games and also used for my fourier-drawing library in python. As soons as i learned python I started following tutorials of C, C++, Java, C#, Swift and I started to get familiar with web development as I wanted to make multiplayer games. Thus, as I entered in higher education, I learned how most of the web is set up and how http works and I learned html, css, javascript, php, sass, bootstrap, nodejs, reactjs, vuejs and django with python (this website uses django). With my newly acquired web skills I started making this website and added few of my games. For my TIPE of the first year i made a replica of the game Othello also called Reversi which can be found here. I also experimented with making games with unity and made an opengl simulation in c++ of granular flow for my TIPE in second year. Then as I always wanted to get seriously into machine learning I followed 5 lessons on Coursera on the matter and i received the certifications that come with it visible on my linkedin page. Meanwhile I started to develop a discord bot for fun to use in my own server. Now i plan to use my deep learning knowledge in order to make it a clever chatbot which you can talk with.

Machine learning

Owner of 5 certificates in deep learning, expert in python, knowledge of Tensorflow, Keras, Numpy, Matplotlib, familiar with R

Web development

Development of this website from scratch in Django (python framework), proficient in Node and Express, knowledge of Vue and React, big fan of MongoDB over MySQL and PostgreSQL, familiar with Sass, extensive use of Bootstrap, grasp of Elasticsearch.

Game creation

Made a 3D game in Unity called Retro Cube, Made multiple game for browser such as SpaceInvader, Connect4, Asteroids, grasp of WebGL and Babylon, Founder of the Pygame-Geometry and Fourier-Drawing libraries on Pypi

Networking and Cyber security

Knowledge of all basics protocols and ports, TCP/IP and OSI models, deep understanding of Unix like systems, aware of MITM, XSS, DDOS, SQL injection attacks, familiar with Postman and Curl, grasp of Wireshark and Nmap
